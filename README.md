# Dismal-Dinner
A "Generational Truth Machine" that uses Python-scraped BEA and BLS data to calculate Real Purchasing Power. Stop comparing salaries and start comparing lifestyles across time (CPI), space (RPP), and policy (Tax Gap).

I plan to solve my favorite personal holiday question.

"My parents got by on $17k in ABCity in 1997. Why am I struggling on $80k in XYZity today?"

Dismal Dinner was built to answer that exact question. Standard inflation calculators are a lie of omission—they tell you how the dollar changed, but they don't tell you how the location or the tax man changed.

This tool collects from the depths of government HTML tables to find the Utility Truth. 

We combine:

The "When" (CPI): Bureau of Labor Statistics historical inflation.

The "Where" (RPP): Bureau of Economic Analysis Regional Price Parities (the "California Premium").

The "Gouge" (Tax Gap): Real-world effective tax rate differentials.

Current Specials (The "Shitty Chef" Specials):

The Gaslight Special: Compare any 1990s midwest salary to a modern HCOL coastal salary.

The Relocation Reality Check: Is that 20% raise in a new city actually a 15% pay cut?
